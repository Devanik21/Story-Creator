## 1. Deep Dive: Emergent Genomic Complexity

The simulation shows immediate, steep pressure toward non-trivial organism design, far surpassing the complexity of the starting **Primordial Goo** (which had an estimated complexity score of <10).

### 1.1. Structural Complexity Breakdown
The average organism has achieved a remarkable level of development for a 20-generation run:
* **Average Complexity:** **32.1** (composite score based on genetic size).
* **Average Cell Count:** **8.5 cells** (up from 1 in the Zygote stage).
* **The Power of Growth:** The evolutionary engine quickly solved the fundamental problem of multi-cellularity.

### 1.2. Cognitive Complexity
The jump in complexity indicates that organisms are not just adding components; they are weaving complex **Genetic Regulatory Networks (GRNs)** to manage new tools like `HIBERNATE`, `MUTATE_SELF`, and **Detonation Logic**. The mere presence of these specialized, high-cost actions proves that life has achieved a cognitive leap sufficient to manage them.

## 3. The Unstable Equilibrium: Detonation vs. Hibernation

The most profound finding is the duality of the **Fitness Landscape**:

* **Aggression Peak:** High fitness is rewarded for organisms that invest heavily in the **DETONATE** action, eliminating competitors quickly.
* **Survival Valley:** The inevitable consequence of widespread detonation is environmental instability and high lethality. This creates an equally high fitness pressure for organisms to evolve **HIBERNATE** capability to simply wait out the massacres.

This creates an extremely hostile, zero-sum game that is unlikely to last.

### 3.1. Missing Complex Behaviors
The following actions have **not yet been discovered or are not favored** by the high-conflict environment:

* **`SYMBIOTE`**: Requires trust and low selection pressure, impossible to evolve when neighbors are exploding.
* **`NETWORK`**: Requires cooperation and stability, not viable when energy pooling results in an immediate target for bombing.
* **`ABSORB`**: Engulfment is a slow process; instant detonation is currently faster.
* **`CAMOUFLAGE`**: May be too resource-intensive or simply hasn't mutated yet.

## 4. Conclusion and Next Steps

The integration of the 18 new complex actions is a resounding success, immediately forcing the emergence of sophisticated strategies. The current universe is locked in a state of **Total Biological Warfare**.
